Assignment name  : rostring
Expected files   : rostring.c
Allowed functions: write, malloc, free
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Write a program that takes a string and displays it after rotating the words
one step to the left (the first word moves to the end). Words in the output
are separated by a single space, followed by a newline.

A word is a section of string delimited by spaces/tabs or by the start/end of
the string.

If the number of parameters is not 1, simply display a newline.

Examples:

$> ./rostring "abc   " | cat -e
abc$
$> ./rostring "Que la      lumiere soit et la lumiere fut"
la lumiere soit et la lumiere fut Que
$> ./rostring "     AkjhZ zLKIJz , 23y"
zLKIJz , 23y AkjhZ
$> ./rostring "first" "2" "11000000"
first
$> ./rostring | cat -e
$
